Product Details



1800 R Curved That Caught Your Eyes

A 1800R curve, almost identical to the human field of view, comes with a different level of immersion than before. As soon as you access the game, it fills your view with the screen. Enjoy realistic play as if you are walking in the real world of the game.

QD-OLED implemented, Incredible Quality

The QLED, which reproduces colors close to real life, clearly shows even the smallest details in the game. The vivid picture quality on the ideal curve brings a new level of reality that you've never felt before.

Dual QHD display

The 5,120x1,440 dual QHD resolution perfectly embodies the game developer’s intended in-game world. With an overwhelming view that seems to connect two monitors, you can predict one step ahead of the enemy.

light-fast reaction rate

Experience a different level of response speed than before. 1 ms response speed and 240 Hz high refresh rate react instantly to your control. It's very advantageous when you play a fast racing game or shooting game.

Leading game to victory

G-Sync compatibility, which synchronizes GPUs and panels, provides smooth play without stuttering, lagging, and delay. AMD FreeSync Premium Pro dramatically reduces aliasing, enabling one step ahead of the enemy.

Details brought to life with HDR

On a dark screen, you can't concentrate or search an enemy. Odyssey G9 offers up to 1000 nits of brightness and HDR10+. It even shows details that were hard to see in bright or dark scenes.

The angle of view that is adjusted as you want

Set the best field of view at eye level. Flexibly adjust to turn, tilt, and move up or down. VESA standard mount makes it easy to install with a stand.

Various ports that connect freely

It has a variety of ports for fast connectivity at any time. Laptops and desktops, as well as smart devices such as mobile and tablets, can be freely to connected. It supports USB 3.0 ports, allowing you to plug any device you need directly into your monitor.
